Crispy Fried Tofu

Crispy tofu is a great way vegan way to enjoy classic Chinese-American cuisine.

Ingredients

  • Oil
  • Tofu (firm or extra firm if possible)
  • Corn starch
  • White pepper
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Coat tofu in cornstarch.
  2. Fry tofu in oil until golden blonde NOT brown.
  3. Coat in salt and white pepper to taste.
  4. Serve with rice
