Chana Masala

A delicious and healthy vegan chickpea curry that is easy to make!

Ingredients

  • Oil
  • Onions
  • Garlic
  • Can of Tomatoes
  • Chickpeas
  • Garam Masala
  • Cumin powder
  • Turmeric powder

Steps

  1. Saute onions on medium heat until soft and tender, then add minced Garlic.
  2. Add 1 tbsp of cumin, turmeric, and garam masala powder.
  3. Add can of tomatoes and chickpeas.
  4. Simmer until chickpeas are soft, season with salt and pepper as desired.
