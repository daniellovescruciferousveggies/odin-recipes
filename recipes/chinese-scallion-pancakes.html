Chinese Scallion Pancakes

An appetizer that is full of scallion flavor! Sure to delight all your guests with a crispy exterior and soft and fluffy interior.

Ingredients

  • Neutral Oil, like vegetable or avocado oil
  • Lots of Green Onions
  • Water
  • All-purpose flour
  • Soy Sauce for dipping

Steps

  1. Saute onions on medium heat until soft and tender, then add minced Garlic.
  2. Add 1 tbsp of cumin, turmeric, and garam masala powder.
  3. Add can of tomatoes and chickpeas.
  4. Simmer until chickpeas are soft, season with salt and pepper as desired.
