Chinese Scallion Pancakes

An appetizer that is full of scallion flavor! Sure to delight all your guests with a crispy exterior and soft and fluffy interior.

Ingredients

  • Neutral Oil, like vegetable or avocado oil
  • Lots of Green Onions
  • Water
  • All-purpose flour
  • Soy Sauce for dipping

Steps

  1. Stir flour whilst pouring in hot water a little at a time
  2. Add cold water and let rest for 1 hour
  3. Heat equal parts oil and flour (Optional: use the white parts of green onion stems to add extra onion flavor to pancake)
  4. Roll out flour, cut into pieces
  5. Let rest
  6. Roll out, put roux mixture, then roll again and flatten (repeat with all pieces)
  7. Fry with oil in pan until golden brown on both sides, serve hot
  8. Dough can be frozen for quick preparation (freeze before the last step)
